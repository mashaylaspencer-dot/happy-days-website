About Us

Happy Days is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating and selling affordable 3D printed toys. Our goal is to bring joy to children while using proceeds to support individuals experiencing homelessness.

Our Mission

We believe that everyone deserves happiness and dignity. By combining creativity, technology, and compassion, we aim to make a meaningful impact in our communities.
